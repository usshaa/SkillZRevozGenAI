DOCUMENT TITLE: Corporate Return and Refund Policy
VERSION: 2.1
EFFECTIVE DATE: January 1, 2026

1. STANDARD RETURN WINDOW
Customers are eligible to return physical merchandise for a full refund within 30 days of the original delivery date. Digital products and software licenses are non-refundable once activated.

2. CONDITIONS FOR RETURN
Items must be returned in their original packaging, unopened, and free of any physical damage. If an item is returned damaged or missing parts, a restocking fee of up to 50% may be applied, or the refund may be denied entirely.

3. REFUND PROCESSING
All refund requests must be accompanied by a valid Order ID (formatted as ORD-XXXXX). Once a return is received and inspected at our fulfillment center, or if a refund is approved for a non-returned item by a supervisor, the funds will be credited back to the original payment method within 3 to 5 business days. 

4. AGENT AUTHORIZATION
Tier 1 Support Agents are not authorized to process refunds exceeding $500 without a Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) supervisor approval gateway.
