DOCUMENT TITLE: Global Shipping and Fulfillment SLA
VERSION: 1.4
EFFECTIVE DATE: March 15, 2025

1. STANDARD SHIPPING TIMELINES
Standard shipping within the continental limits requires 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping guarantees delivery within 2 business days.

2. HANDLING "DELAYED IN TRANSIT" STATUS
If a customer's order status in the CRM is marked as "Delayed in Transit" and the package is more than 3 business days past its original estimated delivery date, the package is considered "Lost."

3. CUSTOMER REMEDIES FOR DELAYS
In the event of a "Lost" package, support personnel must offer the customer one of two options:
Option A: A free replacement shipped via overnight delivery.
Option B: A full cancellation and immediate refund of the original order.

4. ADDRESS CHANGES
Shipping addresses cannot be modified once an order status has transitioned from "Processing" to "Shipped."
