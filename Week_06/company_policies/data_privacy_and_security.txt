DOCUMENT TITLE: Customer Data Privacy & AI Security Guidelines
VERSION: 3.0
EFFECTIVE DATE: May 1, 2026

1. PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION (PII)
To comply with global data privacy regulations, all customer PII must be protected. Support systems, including automated AI chat logs, must actively mask email addresses and phone numbers. For example, user@email.com should be logged as [REDACTED_EMAIL].

2. CRM ACCESS CONTROL
Automated support systems and AI agents operate under a "Read-Only by Default" security posture. Agents may query the CRM using SELECT operations to retrieve order status or customer profiles. 

3. STATE-CHANGING ACTIONS
Any action that modifies the database state (e.g., executing a refund, updating a billing address) must be executed via secure, pre-approved API endpoints. Direct database UPDATE commands by autonomous systems are strictly prohibited.
