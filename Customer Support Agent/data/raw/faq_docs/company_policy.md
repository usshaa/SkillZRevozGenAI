# NovaShop Official Company Policy & FAQ

Welcome to NovaShop! We are committed to providing you with the best electronic gear and customer service. Below is our comprehensive master policy document, outlining our rules and procedures.

## 1. Shipping & Delivery
**Standard Shipping:** All standard orders are shipped within 2-3 business days. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days via standard ground shipping.
**Expedited Shipping:** We offer 2-Day and Overnight shipping options at checkout. Expedited orders placed before 2 PM EST will be processed the same day.
**International Shipping:** We currently ship to the US, Canada, UK, and Australia. International orders may take 10-15 business days and are subject to local customs fees.
**Missing Packages:** If your tracking number shows "Delivered" but you haven't received your package, please check with neighbors and wait 24 hours. If it still hasn't arrived, our agents can assist in filing a claim with the carrier.

## 2. Returns & Refunds
**30-Day Guarantee:** NovaShop accepts returns on all unworn, unused, and unopened electronics within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund to the original payment method.
**Condition Requirements:** Products must be returned in their original packaging with all manuals, cables, and accessories included. Missing accessories will result in a 15% restocking fee.
**Defective Items:** If you receive a defective item, you have 14 days to report it for a free replacement. We will provide a prepaid return label.
**Non-Returnable Items:** Gift cards, software downloads, and clearance items (marked as Final Sale) cannot be returned or exchanged.

## 3. Warranties
**Standard Warranty:** All electronics come with a 1-year limited manufacturer warranty covering hardware defects. This does not cover accidental damage, water damage, or unauthorized modifications.
**NovaProtect Extended Warranty:** Customers can purchase a 2-year or 3-year extended warranty at the time of purchase. This covers accidental drops and spills for laptops, tablets, and headphones.

## 4. Accounts & Loyalty Program
**NovaRewards:** Customers earn 1 point for every $1 spent. 500 points can be redeemed for a $10 discount on future purchases.
**Account Deletion:** You can request account deletion by contacting support. Please note that deleting your account will forfeit any accumulated NovaRewards points.

## 5. Price Matching & Discounts
**Price Match Guarantee:** If you find a lower price on an identical in-stock item at a major authorized retailer (like BestBuy or Amazon), we will match the price. The request must be made within 7 days of your purchase.
**Student & Military Discounts:** We offer a 10% discount for students and active-duty military personnel. Verification is required through ID.me during checkout.

## 6. Pre-Orders & Cancellations
**Pre-Orders:** Payment is charged immediately upon placing a pre-order. Estimated shipping dates are provided on the product page but are subject to change due to manufacturing delays.
**Cancellations:** You can cancel an order within 1 hour of placing it through your account dashboard. After 1 hour, the order enters the fulfillment process and cannot be canceled, though it can be returned once received.

*For any issues not covered in this document, please ask our AI support agent or request an escalation to a human representative.*
